ABOUT US

陶芸工房の有る小さなカフェです。
昭和元年築の祖父の家より
移築の建具や家具や照明と異国のアンティーク。
どこか懐かしいお爺ちゃんちの雰囲気。
フランスの子どもたちの“お友達”を意味する
COPAIN COPINE。
ちょっとお友達のお家へ…そんな感覚で、
心ほぐす時間を
ゆったりと過ごしていただければと願います。

This is a small cafe with a pottery studio.
The fixtures, furniture, and lighting were relocated from my grandfather's house, built in 1926, along with antiques from other countries.
It has the nostalgic atmosphere of a grandfather's house.
COPAIN COPINE means "friend" to children in France.
We hope you can relax and unwind here, feeling as if you're visiting a friend's house.
